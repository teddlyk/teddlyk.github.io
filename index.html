Thodoris Lykouris

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Microsoft Research NYC in the Machine Learning group. I obtained my Ph.D. in the Computer Science department of Cornell University where I had the privilege to be advised by Éva Tardos. Prior to that, I received my Diploma in EECS from National Technical University of Athens.

I am broadly interested in machine learning, algorithms, optimization, and economics. In particular, my research focuses on multiple aspects of data-driven sequential decision making.

My Ph.D. research was supported in part by a Google Ph.D. Fellowship and a Cornell University Fellowship.
